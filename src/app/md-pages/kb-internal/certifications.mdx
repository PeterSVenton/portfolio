# Certifications

This page lists the certifications and professional memberships I hold. Each entry includes a brief description of what it represents and why it is relevant to my work in analytics, engineering and problem solving.

---


# Microsoft DP-600 (Microsoft Certified: Fabric Analytics Engineer Associate)

This certification focuses on building, managing and optimising analytics solutions in Microsoft Fabric. It covers semantic modelling, data transformations, governance, security, and performance tuning across the Fabric ecosystem. The skills align directly with my work in analytics engineering and BI development.

---

# Microsoft DP-700 (Microsoft Certified: Fabric Data Engineer Associate)

This certification demonstrates the ability to design and maintain data engineering workloads inside Microsoft Fabric. It includes experience working with pipelines, notebooks, dataflows, lakehouses and warehouse structures. The qualification reflects the practical work I do building end-to-end data solutions.

---

# Mensa Membership (London)

I am a member of Mensa, the high IQ society. Membership is based on scoring within the top two percent of the population on an approved intelligence assessment. I am part of the Mensa London group, which provides access to events, discussions and a community focused on analytical thinking and problem solving.
